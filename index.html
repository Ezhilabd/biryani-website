A Taste That Tells a Story

Biryani is a popular South Asian dish of fragrant rice layered with spiced meat, such as chicken or mutton, or vegetables, and cooked using the 'dum' or slow-cooking method. Originating from Persia and brought to India, it evolved into many regional variations, including the Hyderabadi style, and is a staple at celebratory occasions.
